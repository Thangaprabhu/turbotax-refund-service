# Where's My Refund status meanings

The IRS tracker presents three primary customer-facing stages: **Return received**, **Refund approved**, and **Refund sent**. Treat the tracker as the authoritative source for federal status. Do not infer that a bank deposit has completed merely because the IRS reports “Refund sent.”
