# Business refund routing playbook

1. Identify entity type and return form.
2. Identify federal versus state authority.
3. Do not apply personal-return timelines automatically.
4. Retrieve business-specific guidance.
5. Route payroll, sales-tax, corporate-income-tax, and partnership matters separately.
