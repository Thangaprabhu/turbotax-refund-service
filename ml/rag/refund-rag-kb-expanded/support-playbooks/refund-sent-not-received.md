# Refund sent but not received playbook

1. Confirm issue date and delivery method.
2. For direct deposit, verify whether the waiting period has elapsed without requesting bank details.
3. For paper checks, retrieve authority-specific lost-check guidance.
4. Recommend a refund trace only when official criteria are met.
5. Never advise filing a duplicate return.
