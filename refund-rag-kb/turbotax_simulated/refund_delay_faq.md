# Simulated TurboTax Help: Refund Delay FAQ

## What should I do if my refund is delayed?

First, check the latest status and last updated time. Refund systems often update once per day or less. If the status says processing and no notice has been issued, the user usually does not need to take immediate action.

## When should I take action?

Take action if:

- The tax authority sent a letter or notice.
- The status asks for identity verification.
- The refund was sent but not received after the expected bank/mail window.
- The refund is outside the published processing timeframe.
- The return was rejected and needs correction.

## Safe answer style

Use: "Based on your current status, the recommended next step is..."
Avoid: "You are guaranteed to receive your refund by..."
