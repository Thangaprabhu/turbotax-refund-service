# New York Refund Status Guidance

New York Department of Taxation and Finance provides an online refund status service. To check status, taxpayers generally need the New York State refund amount requested on the return.

## User guidance

- For New York refunds, use New York-specific status and guidance.
- Explain that state refund processing is separate from federal refund processing.
- If the refund status indicates a letter was sent, the user should respond by the date listed on the letter.
- For amended returns, New York guidance may direct taxpayers to use a phone process rather than the standard online refund status tool.

## Assistant guardrail

Do not show federal-only guidance for a New York state refund unless asked to compare federal and state.
