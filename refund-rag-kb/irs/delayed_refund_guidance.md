# IRS Delayed Refund Guidance

Federal refunds may be delayed for several reasons, including common return mistakes, missing signatures, math errors, Earned Income Credit claims, Additional Child Tax Credit claims, amended returns, injured spouse relief requests, identity verification, fraud review, or manual review.

## User guidance

- Tell the user to check the latest refund status using the IRS Where's My Refund tool or the app experience backed by the refund status service.
- If the status indicates review, explain that some returns require manual or fraud-prevention review.
- If the user received a notice or letter, they should follow the instructions in that notice.
- If no notice was received, they usually do not need to take action immediately.
- For direct deposit refunds marked as sent, remind the user that banks may take additional time and may not process weekends or holidays.

## Assistant guardrail

Do not ask for or display SSN, full bank account number, or sensitive tax details in chat.
